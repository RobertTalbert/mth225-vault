#combinatorics 

## Definition 

> [!tldr] Definition
> For any [[Integers|integer]] $n > 0$, the **factorial** of $n$, denoted $n!$, is the number 
> $$n! = 1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdot \cdots \cdot (n-1) \cdot n$$
> Furthermore we define $0! = 1$.
