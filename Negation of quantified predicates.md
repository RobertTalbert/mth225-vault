## Facts 

> [!tldr] Facts
> Suppose $P(x)$ is a predicate. Then: 
> - The negation of its existentially quantified form $\exists x P(x)$ is $\forall x (\neg P(x))$. 
> - The negation of its universally quantified form $\forall x P(x)$ is $\exists x (\neg P(x))$.
